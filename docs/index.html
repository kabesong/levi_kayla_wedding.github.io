Welcome to our wedding website!

We can barely wait to share our special day with you. We've developed this website as a convenient and interactive way to share all the important information leading up to the wedding. Throughout the site you'll find information on the journey to Alaska, our registry, an introduction to the wedding party, a little on our love story, and more! Lastly, since you are here, we'd like to say thank you for your ongoing love and support. Enjoy the site, we'll see you in Alaska!

When + Where

The ceremony will be held at Gateway to the Arctic in Talkeetna, AK at 2:00pm AKST. Following the ceremony will be happy hour --> dinner --> dancing + games. A formal timeline of the event can be found here.

What to wear

Our wedding will be casual. However, we're less concerned with what you wear and more with how prepared you are for the setting. Alaska's peak snowpack is during March. You can find the forecast here.

Wedding Vibe

YAHTZEE!!! Is the second most important thing we're looking to say at our wedding. The vibe we are going for is a cozy game night feel. There will still be dancing but your lucky die should be packed before your dancing shoes.

The Last Frontier

Not a play on ye old ball n chain...the 49th state is quite the adventure! We've gathered the information for our out of town guests and put them in one place. Accomodations, travel to Talkeetna, and some of our Alaska favorite spots and resources can be found by following the link below.
