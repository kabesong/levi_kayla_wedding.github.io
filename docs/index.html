Welcome to our wedding website!

We've developed this website as a convenient and interactive way to share all the important information leading up to the wedding. Throughout the site you'll find an Alaska guide, our registry, an introduction to the wedding party, a little on our love story, and more! Lastly, since you are here, we'd like to say thank you for your ongoing love and support. Enjoy the site, we hope to see you in Alaska!

When + Where

The ceremony will be held at Gateway to the Arctic in Talkeetna, AK at 2:00pm AKST. It is located ~15 miles south of downtown Talkeetna. Transportation to and from the wedding will be provided from Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge where a room block is reserved. More details on travel to Alaska and how to reserve a room can be found here and a formal timeline of the event here.

What to wear

Our wedding will be semi-formal. However, we're less concerned with what you wear and more with how prepared you are for the setting. Alaska's peak snowpack is during March. Make sure to stay up to date on the forecast.

Wedding Vibe

YAHTZEE!!! Is the second most important thing we're looking to say at our wedding. The vibe we are going for is a cozy game night feel. There will still be dancing but your lucky die should be packed before your dancing shoes. Also fee free to bring your favorite game to play! The ceremony will be UNPLUGGED but to help with your cell phone withdrawl, there will be an open bar.

Virtual Stream

Traveling to Alaska is a LOT. We get it. Even if it is too much, we would still love to have you included in our day! We will be hosting a live stream of the ceremony on Facebook. This will be done via a Facebook Live Event, so if you opt to attend virtually, you will receive a Facebook event invitation (which comes with its own reminders and a direct link to the stream) from Kayla a few weeks in advance.
